Athletics

Athletics remain a cornerstone of student life at MIS as it complements the efforts made inside the classroom. As sports coaches our goals are:
    to prepare students to enter the arena of life. We will stress hard work, leadership, camaraderie, sportsmanship, and fair play along with a desire to learn the intricacies of the activity. Together we will work to solve any problem.
    to ensure that Athletics at MIS is held in high esteem by all stakeholders and that MIS students will enjoy many of these benefits for the rest of their lives.
